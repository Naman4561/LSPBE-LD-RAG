# Final Lessons Learned

- Protocol cleanup changed the conclusion more than another round of model tweaking would have.
- Retrieval-first model selection was the right choice for this project.
- Flat seed retrieval remained stronger than bridge-style local expansion once the protocol was cleaned.
- Bridge seed fairness did matter, but fixing it still did not overturn the final result.
- The answer layer helped explain the results, but it did not become reliable enough to drive the main decision.
- Research repos need a final structural cleanup pass or the truth of the project gets buried under its byproducts.
