# Final Limitations And Future Work

The repo now tells a clean story, but the project still has real limits. The local answer layer is weak, empty predictions remain common, and the final system is better at evidence retrieval than at answer generation. The bridge family was tested seriously, including a fairness repair, but it was not explored forever; a future revisit would need a narrower and better-justified hypothesis.

If work resumes, the best next step is to keep the cleaned protocol fixed and improve answer quality and hard-case retrieval on top of `flat_hybrid_current`. Broad new sweeps should come later, not first.
